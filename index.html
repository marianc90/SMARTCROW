How it works?

The main work consists in two parts, none more important than the other: First, R&I team strips down any trim necessary in order to access to the sheet from below. This work is done with much careful to avoid any kind of sequel. After that, our PDR workers, proceed to push softly the metal from behind to return it to its original shape. No further treatments are required to restore the paint. Then again our R&I restore the trim again, and done. And finally, a buffer for proper termination.

If you have suffered an event of hail in your car/s, please call your insurance and consult if you can manage your repair with us
